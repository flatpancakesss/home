Hack 'n' Slash Touhou fangame where Chen has to defeat as many enemies as possible before time runs out and she wakes up from her daydream.

[東方ゲームジャム2024](https://touhougarakuta.com/news/toho-gamejam-2024/) submission, co-developed with [Jwap](https://x.com/jwapptastic), [Ougi](https://x.com/Ougiikun), and [Redi](https://x.com/redi_rkgk). Now updated with Endless Mode and online leaderboards.

Play [here](https://jwapobie.itch.io/march-of-the-beast).

#### Released 20 October 2024.
